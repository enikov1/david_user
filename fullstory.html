About this villa

In the beautiful place of El’Perello, a town of beautiful traditions and customs, within a protected natural park is this magnificent property with excellent views of the sea, the mountains and the Ebro Delta. Land of 3.5 hectares. Impressive design villa with an area of ​​325m2, which has just undergone an exclusive luxury interior renovation. In this spacious and bright property with dry stone and teak wood walls, all rooms enjoy a lot of natural light. It has 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms, it has a spacious modern kitchen and magnificent open-plan reception room with access to a fantastic terrace with stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea. Next, we will find several living rooms and terraces to rest with fantastic views. Finally, I want to emphasize that this exceptional design villa also has all the comfort and excellent qualities of materials and finishes, modern lighting, central heating with firewood, fireplace, 78,000-liter water tank and installation of high-quality solar panels. Quality for electricity, generator. In addition, it is only 6 km from the town of El Perello with all the necessary services and 8 km from the magnificent beaches.

The south facing villa offers high quality finishes throughout and the extensive accommodation comprises of an entrance hall, impressive lounge and dining area, open plan fully fitted kitchen, guest cloakroom, five bedroom all with en-suite bathrooms, Sauna, Turkish Bath, Chill out areas, Solarium and a four car garage.

Surrounded by a landscaped garden with a kidney shaped swimming pool, Jacuzzi, BBQ area and a Paddle court. Features include a lift to all floors, electric blinds, underfloor heating throughout.
