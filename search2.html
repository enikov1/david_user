Property facts for Almeria province

How much does property cost to buy in Almeria Province?

The average property price in Almeria Province is around €145,000 which is 58% lower than the national average in Spain

Average property prices:
Who buys in Almeria Province?

Of the 2,893 homes sold in the last quarter, 26% were bought by international buyers, this is 1.5 times the average in Spain. The majority of expats living in Almeria Province are Romanian followed by British and Bulgarian.

Where are the cheapest & most expensive places to buy in Almeria Province?

Throughout the whole of Almeria Province properties are cheaper on average than in many other Spanish locations. The most expensive areas include the pretty whitewashed town of Mojácar and San Juan De Los Terreros, both in stunning seaside locations. The most affordable property can be found in the quaint hillside town of Lubrin, lesser known town of Oria and small seaside town of Vera.
